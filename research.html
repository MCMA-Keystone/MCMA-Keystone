Research and Projects

Welcome, curious wanderer! You may notice something funny with this page: it's filled with blogposts, not published, formal, professional-looking articles! It is perhaps true that we're not as professional as many research institutions, however the main purpose is to make our projects and ideas easily understandable to everyone. Our wording and structure is informal, our text is colorful, and our posts contain no professional jargon.

In addition to projects, some of the posts available here are summaries of our research, as well as future plans/goals. Sometimes, we will write about interesting research published by other people.

Final note for readers: We welcome (and strongly encourage) questions and comments from both experts and the general public. We will carefully consider all suggestions and give detailed responses to all questions during the MCMA's conferences.
