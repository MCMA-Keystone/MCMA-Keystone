Research and Projects

Welcome, curious wanderer! You may notice something funny with this page: it's filled with blogposts, not published, formal, professional-looking research papers! It is perhaps true that we're not as professional as many research institutions, however the main purpose is to make our research and ideas easily understandable to everyone. Our wording and structure is informal, our text is colorful, and our posts contain no professional jargon.

In addition to research, most of the posts available here are more detailed explanations and descriptions of our events, as well as future plans/goals. You can think of this as part of our research as well.

Final note for readers: We welcome (and strongly encourage) questions and comments from both experts and the general public. We will carefully consider all suggestions and give detailed responses to all questions during the MCMA's conferences.
