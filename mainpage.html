This is the official website of the

Mani-Cretin Mathematics Association (MCMA)


Introduction

The MCMA is an organization founded by Tata Li and directed by Kevin Shi. It is sponsored and supported by the Academy of Cretins, Cretin Organization, Mani Organization, Mani University, and Giberland Philharmonic. MCMA hosts mathematics contests such as the Mani-Cretin Mathematics Examination (MCME), the Mani-Cretin Open Mathematics Tournament (MCOMT), and Mani-Cretin Mathematics Talent Search (MCMST).
