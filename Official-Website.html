Mani-Cretin Mathematics Association (Keystone)

Introduction

MCMA is an organization founded by Tata Li and directed by Kevin Shi; it is sponcered by Academy of Cretin, Cretin Organization, Mani Organization, Mani University. MCMA hosts mathematics contests such as the Mani-Cretin Mathematics Examination (MCME) and the Mani-Cretin Invitational Mathematics Examination (MCIME).
